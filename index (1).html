What I Work On

Research Areas

🔬

PFAS Sensing & Environmental Chemistry

Developing emulsion-based PFAS sensor platforms achieving parts-per-trillion detection of PFOA in real environmental water samples. Designing PFAS-responsive diblock copolymer receptors with fluoroalkyl-fluoroalkyl molecular recognition.

🧪

Complex Emulsions & Colloidal Chemistry

Engineering detergent-free complex emulsion systems, studying interfacial phenomena, colloidal dynamics, emulsion rheology and phase behavior. Pioneered self-stabilized emulsions using amphiphilic α-cyanostilbene fluorophores.

⚗️

Polymer Synthesis & Functional Materials

Synthesizing diblock copolymers (PS-b-P4VP, PGMA), organic fluorophores, and stimuli-responsive polymer systems. Designing supramolecular receptors for selective analyte detection.

🔧

Microfluidics & Device Fabrication

Designing pressure-driven microfluidic devices for monodisperse droplet generation, controlled emulsification, and on-chip real-time sensing. Fabricating PDMS and glass-based chips with integrated optical readout.
